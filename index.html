FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Does getting a quote cost anything?
No. Our quotes are completely free and come with zero obligation to enroll. You can see your personalized rates and make an informed decision without any pressure whatsoever.
Will my energy be interrupted when I switch?
Never. The switch is purely administrative — energy delivery remains constant through the same infrastructure. You will not experience any downtime, outages, or service disruptions of any kind.
What if I change my mind after enrolling?
Our plans allow customers to cancel service without termination fees. You are not locked in. If you want to leave, we make it easy.
Are there any hidden charges or fees?
We disclose all pricing terms upfront before you sign. All fees, rates, and contract details are clearly stated — no surprises and no fine print.
Which states do you currently serve?
We currently offer plans in Delaware, Indiana, Massachusetts, Michigan, New Jersey, Ohio, Pennsylvania, Rhode Island, Virginia, and Washington D.C. Enter your zip code above to see rates available in your area.
